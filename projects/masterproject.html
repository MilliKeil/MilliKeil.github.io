cadavre construit

2026, performative web-game, masterproject

Cadavre Construit is playfully working with the premise that AI-generated confabulations can be seen as productive irritations for conceptual theatre processes. Inspired by the surrealist game of "cadavre exquis" (exquisite corpse), the project explores the co-creation of a previously non-existent reality in the form of a short play through productive co-confabulation.
