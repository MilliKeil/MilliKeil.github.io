autoimmunität

2023, speculative game, joint project with Aron Petau and Marla Gaiser

Autoimmunität (Autoimmunity) is a term for defects, that are produced by a dysfunctional self-tolerance of a system. This dysfunction causes the immune system to stop accepting certain parts of itself and build antibodies instead.
A playful interactive experience inspired by the protests of “Last Generation” (activist group) to reflect on the societal value of the car.
