klasse klima kleber

2023, design of a stickerset for klasse klima

The designed sticker set may serve to imprint the "klasse klima," make a political statement, sticker over undesirable stickers, find like-minded people, generate attention, or simply contribute to a sticker wall. We aim to provide a platform for climate justice, especially after the pandemic, allowing life to transition from the digital realm back to analog space!
