milli keil

Experimenting at the intersection of Design, New Media and Performance.

With a background in climate policy-making (including a peer-reviewed publication) — I now explore the symbiotic potential of design and computation methods. Since 2022, I have been pursuing the joint masters program Design & Computation between the University of the Arts Berlin and the Technical University Berlin, while also fighting for climate justice together with Klasse Klima. I expanded my artistic practice during a guest semester in the Intermedia Studio at the Academy of Fine Arts and Design Bratislava. Since 2023, I am a scholarship recipient by the Studienstiftung des Deutschen Volkes.

My work bridges performing arts, digital media, and societal discourse, often through participatory and experimental approaches. I also worked as an artistic researcher together with the theatre collective Rimini Protokoll for the production Die Zeitmaschine at Staatstheater Cottbus and regularly work as an extra at the Deutsche Oper Berlin.
